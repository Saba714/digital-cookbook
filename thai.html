Thai Recipes

Pad Thai

Ingredients:

  • 200g Rice Noodles
  • Fresh Shrimp
  • 2 Eggs (lightly beaten)
  • Bean Sprouts & Peanuts
  • Pad Thai Sauce (Tamarind based)

Method:

1. Soak rice noodles in warm water until soft.
2. Stir-fry shrimp in a hot pan until cooked.
3. Push ingredients to the side, scramble the eggs, then mix in noodles.
4. Add sauce and bean sprouts, toss quickly, and top with crushed peanuts!

Mango Sticky Rice

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Glutinous (sticky) rice
  • 1/2 cup Coconut milk
  • 1/4 cup Sugar
  • 1 Ripe Yellow Mango
  • Pinch of Salt

Method:

1. Steam the sticky rice until soft and translucent.
2. Heat coconut milk, sugar, and salt together until dissolved.
3. Pour the mixture over the warm rice and let it sit for 20 minutes.
4. Serve the sweet rice with fresh mango slices on the side!

Som Tum

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Shredded green papaya
  • 2 Garlic cloves
  • 5 Cherry tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp Fish sauce & 1 tbsp Lime juice
  • 1 tbsp Roasted peanuts

Method:

1. Use a mortar and pestle to pound garlic and chilies together.
2. Add tomatoes, long beans, and peanuts, lightly bruising them.
3. Mix in the fish sauce, lime juice, and palm sugar.
4. Toss in the shredded papaya and serve chilled!

Green Thai Curry

Ingredients:

  • 400ml Coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp Green curry paste
  • 300g Chicken breast (sliced)
  • 1/2 cup Thai Eggplant or Bamboo shoots
  • Fresh Basil & Red chili

Method:

1. Fry the curry paste in a little coconut milk until fragrant.
2. Add chicken and stir-fry until coated in paste.
3. Pour in the rest of the coconut milk and add vegetables.
4. Simmer until chicken is cooked, then garnish with fresh basil!

Tom Yum Goong

Ingredients:

  • 500g Large Shrimp
  • 2 stalks Lemongrass (bruised)
  • 3-4 Kaffir lime leaves
  • 2 tbsp Thai chili paste (Nam Prik Pao)
  • Mushrooms & Lime juice

Method:

1. Boil water with lemongrass, galangal, and lime leaves to create a fragrant base.
2. Add mushrooms and chili paste, simmering for 2 minutes.
3. Add shrimp and cook until they turn pink.
4. Turn off heat, stir in lime juice and fish sauce, and serve immediately!
