Italian Recipes

Fettuccine Alfredo

Ingredients:

  • 500g Fettuccine pasta
  • 1 cup Heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup Unsalted butter
  • 1 cup Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh Parsley & Black pepper

Method:

1. Boil the pasta in salted water until al dente.
2. Melt butter and cream in a pan over medium heat.
3. Stir in Parmesan cheese until the sauce is smooth and thick.
4. Toss the pasta in the sauce and garnish with fresh parsley!

Margherita Pizza

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough base
  • 1/2 cup Tomato sauce
  • 200g Fresh Mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh Basil leaves
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil

Method:

1. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the rolled-out dough.
2. Add slices of fresh mozzarella across the surface.
3. Bake in a very hot oven until the crust is golden and cheese bubbles.
4. Top with fresh basil leaves and a drizzle of olive oil before serving!

Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • 400g Spaghetti
  • 150g Pancetta or Guanciale
  • 3 large Egg yolks
  • 1 cup Pecorino Romano cheese
  • Black pepper

Method:

1. Boil spaghetti; meanwhile, fry the pancetta until crispy.
2. Whisk egg yolks and cheese together in a small bowl.
3. Toss hot pasta with pancetta, then remove from heat.
4. Quickly stir in the egg mixture with a splash of pasta water for a creamy finish!

Creamy Mushroom Risotto

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 cups Arborio Rice
  • 500g Sliced Mushrooms
  • 4 cups Vegetable broth (warm)
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tbsp Butter & 1 Onion

Method:

1. Saute onions and mushrooms in butter until soft.
2. Add rice and toast it for 2 minutes.
3. Add broth one ladle at a time, stirring until absorbed.
4. Once rice is creamy, stir in Parmesan and serve immediately!

Tiramisu

Ingredients:

  • 1 pack Ladyfinger biscuits
  • 2 cups Mascarpone cheese
  • 1 cup Strong Espresso (cooled)
  • 2 tbsp Cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup Sugar

Method:

1. Whisk mascarpone with sugar until light and fluffy.
2. Quickly dip ladyfingers in espresso and layer them in a dish.
3. Spread the mascarpone cream over the biscuits.
4. Repeat layers, dust with cocoa powder, and chill for 4 hours!
