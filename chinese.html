Chinese Recipes

Kung Pao Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 500g Chicken (diced)
  • Roasted Peanuts
  • Dried Red Chilies
  • Soy Sauce & Vinegar
  • Scallions & Garlic

Method:

1. Marinate chicken in soy sauce and cornstarch for 10 minutes.
2. Stir-fry the chicken in a hot wok until cooked through.
3. Add chilies, garlic, and scallions, then pour in the sauce mixture.
4. Stir in the peanuts and serve hot with steamed rice!

Egg Fried Rice

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups Cold cooked rice
  • 3 Eggs (beaten)
  • 1 cup Frozen vegetables
  • 2 tbsp Soy sauce & 1 tbsp Sesame oil
  • Chopped Garlic

Method:

1. Scramble eggs in a wok and set them aside.
2. Sauté garlic and vegetables until tender.
3. Add the rice and break up any clumps while stirring.
4. Stir in soy sauce, sesame oil, and eggs until evenly mixed!

Beef and Broccoli

Ingredients:

  • 400g Beef steak (thinly sliced)
  • 2 cups Broccoli florets
  • 1/4 cup Oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp Ginger (grated)
  • 1 tsp Cornstarch

Method:

1. Marinate beef in a little soy sauce and cornstarch.
2. Sear the beef in a hot wok and remove.
3. Stir-fry broccoli and ginger with a splash of water.
4. Add beef back with oyster sauce and stir until thick and glossy.

Vegetable Chow Mein

Ingredients:

  • 200g Egg noodles
  • 1 cup Shredded cabbage
  • 1 Bell pepper (sliced)
  • 2 tbsp Soy sauce & 1 tsp Sugar
  • 1 tbsp Oil

Method:

1. Boil noodles until soft and drain.
2. Stir-fry cabbage and peppers on high heat for 2 minutes.
3. Add noodles to the wok with soy sauce and sugar.
4. Toss everything together quickly so the noodles don't stick!

Sweet and Sour Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 500g Chicken (battered and fried)
  • 1/2 cup Pineapple chunks
  • 1/4 cup Ketchup & 1/4 cup Vinegar
  • 1/4 cup Sugar
  • Onions and Bell peppers

Method:

1. Mix ketchup, vinegar, and sugar in a bowl to make the sauce.
2. Sauté onions, peppers, and pineapple in a pan.
3. Pour in the sauce and bring to a simmer.
4. Toss in the crispy chicken until coated and serve with rice!
